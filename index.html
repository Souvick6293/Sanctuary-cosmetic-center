About Us

The board-certified providers at Sanctuary Cosmetic Center specialize in oculofacial cosmetic eyelid and facial surgery, CoolSculpting®, chemical peels, dermal fillers, and Botox® and Dysport® injections, and much more. The plastic surgeons provide outpatient surgery in a safe, private, AAAHC-certified environment.

Services at Sanctuary Cosmetic Center

Patients seeking surgical or nonsurgical procedures to improve the appearance of their face, chest, abdomen, breasts, butt, or entire body can turn to Sanctuary Cosmetic Center for premium care. Treatments exceed expected outcomes by achieving a natural (not plastic), youthful look that turns back the clock without appearing made up.
